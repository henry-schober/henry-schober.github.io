About Me!

A recent master’s degree recipient in Genetics and Genomic sciences with both passion and experience in bioinformatic processes involving genomic information, that includes experiences with developing sequencing pipelines, genome assembly pipelines, and quality control pipelines using Linux and Nextflow. Strong Background with R, Bash, and Python in regard to data analysis of biological data. Proven ability to collaborate across both dry lab and wet lab practices due to experience behind both a computer and a wet lab bench, as well as a proven ability to present research at genomic conventions.
